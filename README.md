# S25 Work Term Report — BMO Financial Group

Work term report for my Summer 2025 co-op placement as a **Technology Research Analyst** at BMO Financial Group.

Built with [Next.js 15](https://nextjs.org), [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com), and [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org).

## Overview

A single-page report covering:

- **Company Profile** — BMO Financial Group and the Technology Innovation team
- **Job Description** — Role responsibilities, skills, and projects shipped
- **Learning Goals** — Five co-op learning goals with action plans and reflections
- **Conclusion** — Key learnings and what comes next
- **Acknowledgements** — The people who made the term possible

## Live

[https://s25-bmo.vercel.app](https://s25-bmo.vercel.app)
